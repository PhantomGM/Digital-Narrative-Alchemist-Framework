# Golarion World Lore

Golarion is a planet in the Material Plane, the center of the Pathfinder universe. It is a world of magic, monsters, gods, and ancient ruins.

## Major Regions
- **Absalom**: The City at the Center of the World. Founded by the living god Aroden. It houses the Starstone, a meteorite that can grant divinity to those who survive its test.
- **The Inner Sea Region**: The primary setting, encompassing the continents of Avistan (north) and Garund (south), surrounding the Inner Sea.
- **Cheliax**: An empire formerly dedicated to Aroden, now ruled by diabolists who struck a bargain with Hell after Aroden's death to maintain power.
- **Varisia**: A frontier land of ancient Thassilonian ruins, home to the Varisian and Shoanti people.
- **The Mwangi Expanse**: A vast, vibrant jungle continent in central Garund, featuring advanced city-states, ancient magic, and fierce independence.

## Recent History
The "present day" in Golarion is 472X AR (Absalom Reckoning). A defining event of the modern age was the Death of Aroden over a century ago. Prophecy failed when he died, plunging the world into the Age of Lost Omens.

## Magic and Gods
Gods are real, active, but rarely intervene directly. Core deities include Iomedae (goddess of valor), Sarenrae (goddess of redemption and the sun), Pharasma (goddess of death and fate), and Asmodeus (god of tyranny and Hell).
Magic is divided into four traditions:
- **Arcane**: Logical, intellectual magic (Wizards).
- **Divine**: Power granted by faith and deities (Clerics).
- **Primal**: Instinctual magic tied to nature (Druids).
- **Occult**: Bizarre, esoteric magic of the mind and spirit (Bards).
