# Tone Guide for Pathfinder 2E

Pathfinder 2nd Edition's tone is **Heroic High Fantasy**. 

## Key Elements
1. **Heroic Scale**: Player Characters are exceptional individuals. While level 1 characters might struggle against giant rats, level 20 characters wrestle demigods and reshape reality.
2. **Consequences Matter**: Choices have weight. The game features tactical depth that rewards preparation and punishes reckless action. Failure is an option, and death is a tangible threat without proper teamwork.
3. **Diverse and Inclusive**: Golarion hosts a massive diversity of cultures, ancestries, and perspectives. Characters of any background, orientation, or identity have a place in the narrative.
4. **Age of Lost Omens**: The overarching theme is uncertainty. Because prophecy failed with Aroden's death, the future is unwritten. The PCs are the masters of their own destiny.
5. **Teamwork Over Individual Mechanics**: Pathfinder 2E mechanically enforces teamwork. A PC who buffs an ally or debuffs an enemy (via the 3-action economy) is far more effective than a lone wolf.
